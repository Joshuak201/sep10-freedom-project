ABOUT

What will this project be about?

Astrophysics is the use of chemistry and real-world physics to further understand the universe around us. What role does technology play into astrophysics, you may ask, well, there are many forms of technology that play into it. Ranging from telescopes, satellites, space probes, or even software like IRAF, ds9, and CASA. What are these you may ask, well, this project will let you know exactly what these technologies are.

SOFTWARE

IRAF

IRAF(also known as "Image Reduction and Analysis Facility")is used by astronomers to analyze data that takes place in space.

DS9

DS9 is an application used for viewing and analyzing data that takes place in space.

CASA

CASA(also known as "Common Astronomy Software Applications") is a software used for processing and analyzing radio astronomical data, especially from telescopes.

HARDWARE

Telescope

Telescopes are the most common hardware in all of space exploration, allowing scientists to observe and gather data from across the universe.

Satellite

Satellites are used to monitor both Earth and space, collect data, and relay communications, which makes its role in space exploration all the more important.

Space Probe

Space probes are unmanned spacecrafts that used to explore space and gather information about planets, moons, and other things in the universe.
